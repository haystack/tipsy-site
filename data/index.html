About

Tipsy is a browser extension that will allow you to support online content. Just install the app and keep browsing as you always do. Tipsy will then remind you to donate to the creators of the content you like, giving more to the ones you liked the best.

Getting Paid

If you create content you can start getting paid right away. All you need to do is include a link tag in the head of your site with rel="author" and name="Tipsy" attributes. You can then include the payment processors with whom you would accept payment. You would insert something looking like this:

<link rel="author" name="Tipsy" paypal="tipsy@haystack.com">

After you do this, any user who has Tipsy installed will automatically be generating payments based on browsing activity on your site! For particular payment processors Tipsy uses different formats:

  • PayPal:

    For now, just include your PayPal email address, just like in the example above. We are working on another method.

  • Dwolla:

    In order to accept Dwolla payments you will need to include your key in the author tag. In order to generate this key you need to create an account with dwolla. Then you need to generate a button. Don't worry too much about all the different fields or button types - it doesn't matter since all we need is the key. Once you've created the button, go back to this page. There, you should see your key. This is the key that would be in any button you place on a website, that would be visible to anyone lookig at the source. You can then include this as an attribute in the link tag described above like so: dwolla="kRrCD32hkwH0E7ldn/xeI/WCQKqR2dVPLuQjF6Bu/g4OlMnZa9".

The Log Page

Tipsy keeps a log of the pages you visited and how much time you spent on then. Content providers will have to place some author and payment information in their source so Tipsy can know how to pay them. See the source of this page for an example.

The Settings Page

You can configure how Tipsy will calculate contribution recommendations and how Tipsy will remind you to pay.

Donations Page

Tipsy will calculate a suggested donation and you can select to pay using the options provided. You can, of course, pay as much or as little as you want. Tipsy does not collect any money - you will be directed to the chosen payment processor's website.
